# Scenario: Product Vision & Roadmap

## Target
Create the foundational product vision document for Hexalith FrontShell. Without a clear vision, contributors lack strategic direction and improvement efforts are ad hoc.

## Current State
No documented product vision. The codebase speaks through its architecture (micro-frontend shell, CQRS, plugin modules) but there is no single document that articulates purpose, audience, priorities, or roadmap.

## Desired State
A concise product vision document in `design-artifacts/A-Product-Brief/` that any contributor or evaluator can read to understand what Hexalith FrontShell is, who it's for, and where it's going.

## Success Criteria
1. Vision statement exists and is clear
2. Strategic priorities are defined and ranked
3. Module roadmap with current and planned capabilities
4. Document is actionable — guides the next 4 improvement targets
5. Lives in `design-artifacts/A-Product-Brief/product-vision.md`

## Scope
- Pages affected: None (documentation only)
- Components touched: None
- Data changes: None
- Risk level: Low
